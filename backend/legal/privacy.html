BytzGo Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

BytzGo (“we”, “our”) operates the BytzGo mobile app and website at bytzgo.net for on-demand delivery and courier services in Ghana.

Information we collect

  • Account: name, email, phone number, password (stored hashed), and role (customer, rider, vendor, or admin).
  • Location: GPS coordinates when you book a delivery, track a trip, or go online as a rider — only while you use those features.
  • Orders: pickup and drop-off addresses, order details, payment references, and chat messages related to active trips.
  • Vendor content: shop details and product photos you upload.
  • Rider verification: licence, Ghana card, and profile photo images for KYC review.
  • Device: push notification tokens (FCM) to send ride and order alerts.

How we use information

  • Provide delivery matching, live tracking, maps, and payments.
  • Send transactional notifications (new jobs, order updates, chat).
  • Operate admin and vendor tools; prevent fraud and abuse.
  • Improve reliability and support (support@bytzgo.com).

Sharing

We share trip-related data between customers, assigned riders, and relevant vendors for fulfilment. We use service providers including Google (Maps, Sign-In, Firebase Cloud Messaging), Paystack (payments), and cloud hosting (Render). We do not sell personal data.

Retention

Order records may be kept for accounting and dispute resolution. You may request account deletion; we remove or anonymise personal data when no active orders block deletion.

Security

Data is transmitted over HTTPS. Access to production systems is restricted. No method is 100% secure; contact us if you suspect unauthorised access.

Your choices

  • Deny location or notification permissions (some features will not work).
  • Update profile details in the app.
  • Delete your account in Profile → Delete account, or email support@bytzgo.com.

Children

BytzGo is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
