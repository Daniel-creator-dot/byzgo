## Full description

```
BytzGo is Ghana’s on-demand delivery platform — book a bike courier, track your trip live, and order from local vendors in one app.

FOR CUSTOMERS
• Request a bike delivery with pickup and drop-off on the map
• Live trip tracking and rider ETA
• Shop partner stores and pharmacies
• Secure trip PIN and in-app chat

FOR RIDERS
• Go online and receive nearby delivery jobs
• Turn-by-turn navigation and earnings wallet
• Document verification for trusted fleet

FOR VENDORS
• List your store and upload menu items with photos
• Manage orders from the BytzGo network

Built for Accra and cities across Ghana. Sign in with phone or Google. Support: support@bytzgo.com

Privacy: https://www.bytzgo.net/privacy
```
